Access Control

Access control is a security technique that is used to regulate and manage access to a physical or digital resource. It involves the use of various mechanisms and technologies to control who can enter a specific area, use certain resources or access certain information. A firm that provides access control services typically offers a range of solutions to help their clients secure their premises and manage access to various areas of their building or property. These solutions may include:

  1. Biometric access control: This involves the use of biometric technologies such as fingerprint, facial recognition, or iris scanning to identify individuals and grant or deny access to certain areas or resources.
